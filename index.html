What is numerology?

Numbers tell a story

Numerology is a millennia-old wisdom tradition that sees numbers as carriers of energy and meaning. By reducing your date of birth and full name to core numbers from 1 to 9, a map of your strengths, challenges and life themes is revealed.

Each number has its own distinct character — from the independent force of number 1 to the wisdom and compassion of number 9. Together they form your personal numerological diamond: a unique imprint of your life's potential.
